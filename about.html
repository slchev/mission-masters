Three returned missionaries
eager to help...

Kevin, Spencer, and Steven, formerly known as Elder Salas, Elder Hart and Elder Chevrier met while 2017 serving as full time missionaries in the Peru. These three young men poured their hearts and souls into missionary work. There was nothing that brought them greater joy than helping other make changes in their lives to come unto Christ and live more purely.
Unfortunately, along the way it wasn’t all sunshine and sparkles, living in another country far from home presented a multitude of challenges. Upon returning home from their missions, they couldn’t bear the thought of giving up the Lord’s work. So, they decided to dedicate their time and efforts into helping your child know what he or she will need to best serve the Lord as a missionary!
